A Place of Peace & Welcome

Our open churches provide a sanctuary of peace, comfort, and spiritual refuge — whenever you need it most.

Peace & Prayer

Find quiet spaces for prayer, meditation, and reflection. Light a candle, sit in silence, or simply breathe.

Safety & Welcome

Every person is welcomed with open arms. Our churches are safe havens where you belong exactly as you are.

Support & Care

Compassionate volunteers and clergy are available to listen, pray with you, or simply offer a comforting presence.

God's Doors Should Never Be Closed

A movement calling on churches everywhere to keep their doors open 24/7 for anyone in need, for any reason. Because God's house should be the safest, most welcoming place in the community — at all times.

This Is Deeper Than Homelessness

Every hour of every day, someone in your community needs a safe place. Here's why keeping our doors open 24/7 can change — and save — lives.

Crisis of Faith

Someone might be suicidal and need prayer, a safe place to think, or just someone to listen. In their darkest hour, God's house could be the light that saves them.

Family Conflict

Someone fighting with family needs space to cool down, pray, and find peace. A sanctuary should always be available when home doesn't feel safe.

Addiction Battle

Someone struggling with addiction needs immediate help and a place to resist temptation. Recovery happens one moment at a time — we should be there for every one.

Need for Prayer

Someone just needs to sit in peace, cry, or pray in God's presence. Sometimes silence in a sacred space is the most powerful medicine.

Safety & Protection

Someone escaping danger needs immediate shelter and protection. Churches should be known as the safest places in every community.

Divine Encounter

Someone seeking God might feel called to visit at 3 AM. We should never let locked doors prevent a divine encounter.

Our Vision for Revival

We are building a powerful network of open churches, united in revival, returning to the radical hospitality that defined the early church.

Always Open

Church doors should never be closed to those in need. We believe this simple act can transform communities.

Powerful Network

Together, we're creating a safety net across communities — a network of hope that spans every neighborhood.

Revival Spirit

This movement is about returning to our calling — to be the hands and feet of Jesus in our communities.

Join the Network

Calling on pastors, leaders, and congregations to join us in opening their doors. Together, we can create a movement that changes everything.
